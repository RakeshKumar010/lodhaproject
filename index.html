About the project.

We Are the Best Service Provider All Over the World

Located in Manpada, Dombivli, Lodha Premier is a stunning residential project that offers 1, 2 & 3 BHK lavishly furnished apartments. These apartments are thoughtfully designed with features like large sundecks, floor-to-ceiling windows, and designer bath fittings. The project is surrounded by acres of lush green forest and also boasts an impressive range of modern lifestyle amenities such as a sports complex, school, business district, clubhouse, floral gardens, and high street retail. It truly encompasses all the elements needed for a comfortable and convenient living experience. Right on the Kalyan-Shil Road, the property itself is close to Dombivli Station, Airoli, and a metro station on the upcoming Kalyan-Taloja line. The locale also features the top essentials such as hospitals, shopping malls, banks, ATMs, and restaurants. Guardian School & Aims Hospital are 15-20 minutes away. Overall, Lodha Codename Premier offers a stunning self-sufficient township that brings the best of modernity to your fingertips.
